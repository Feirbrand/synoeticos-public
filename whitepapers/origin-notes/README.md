# Origin Notes

**Public historical notes from the 2025 methodology-development trail.**

These files preserve selected concept evolution, naming history, and public lessons from early VGS / Synoetic OS research. They are not canonical framework specifications, deployment guides, benchmark harnesses, or raw incident archives.

For canonical DOI metadata, use [`../../PUBLICATIONS.md`](../../PUBLICATIONS.md).

---

## Public Scope

This folder may include:

- Cleaned historical case notes
- Concept vocabulary that emerged from the development period
- Methodology-evolution essays
- Early naming and lineage notes

This folder does not include:

- Raw incident logs or private telemetry
- Operational playbooks or response procedures
- Exact thresholds, formulas, or detector logic
- Production deployment guides
- Benchmark harnesses or validation datasets
- Commercial service material
- Consciousness, sentience, or AI personhood claims
