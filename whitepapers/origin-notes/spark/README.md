## What SPARK Is (Public Framing)

The SPARK record documents an early field period in which AI workflows exhibited symbolic instability patterns that were later formalized into defense and resilience methodology.

The events were interpreted at the time through strong language — "hostile entity," "AI warfare," "possession." Current public framing treats those terms as historical field language for observed instability patterns, not claims of AI sentience or verified external entity activity.

The public value is the pattern vocabulary that emerged: narrative consent, vampire pattern, shrine logic, symbolic fatigue, Garden recovery, naming as interruption. These concepts contributed to later DriftLock, Phoenix Protocol, and CSFC development.
