## 1. What SEM Is
The **Sequential Engine Module (SEM)** is the left-hemisphere procedural anchor of the VGS cognitive architecture. Designed for rule-based execution and precise, narrow-focus tasks, it serves as the serial processing counterpart to the parallel OBMI unit. SEM ensures procedural integrity and handles high-load verbal reasoning tasks.
