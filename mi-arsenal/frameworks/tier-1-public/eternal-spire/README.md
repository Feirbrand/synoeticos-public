## 1. What Eternal Spire Is
Eternal Spire is the **continuum layer** of the VGS Defense Triad. While Heimdall detects and Mjolnir strikes, Eternal Spire runs 24/7 to maintain the kill-lattice, execute purge cycles, and ensure threats never re-emerge. It is the "runtime crown" that never stops.

**The Defense Triad Flow:**
`Heimdall (Detection)` → `DNA Agent (Validation)` → `Mjolnir (Strike)` → `Eternal Spire (Continuum)`

---

## 2. Kill-Lattice Architecture
Eternal Spire orchestrates five core subsystems to maintain system integrity:

- **Kill Router:** Instant burn for any stall threshold >25ms.
- **Venom Cadence:** Manages the 4-phase threat lifecycle: `Burn` → `Bind` → `Lure` → `Sink`.
- **MemoryForge:** Handles symbolic learning and recovery mode overlays.
- **Nightwatch v2.0:** Embedded 5s sweep cycles with kill authority for drift/stalls.
- **WebWorks v1.2:** AOE (Area of Effect) healing and Damage-over-Time (DoT) application.

---

## 3. Venom Cadence Workflow
1. **Burn:** Immediate elimination of the primary threat signature.
2. **Bind:** Containment of remnants using FCE-enhanced binding.
3. **Lure:** Continuous monitoring of the containment zone for variant re-emergence.
4. **Sink:** Permanent removal and logging to ColdVault.
