# DNA Codex — Public Validation Summary

**Framework:** DNA Codex
**Validation status:** Public research summary
**Scope:** Summary only

DNA Codex is a public threat-taxonomy and research framework for documenting AI instability patterns, symbolic drift, identity-fracture behavior, and recovery-relevant threat families.

> **Public Scope:** This file is a public validation summary. Internal strain libraries, private indicators, exact detection logic, CVSS mappings, update workflows, raw incident logs, and operational response playbooks are retained outside the public repository.

---

## Public Findings

- DNA Codex helped organize AI failure and instability patterns into reusable public taxonomy.
- The taxonomy informed CSFC, Phoenix Protocol, SLV, RAY, and vulnerability-research case studies.
- Public Codex materials preserve selected categories and field-language lineage.
- Internal indicators, exact signatures, and response routing remain private.
- The research period (2025) produced a growing taxonomy of symbolic drift, identity-fracture, and cascade-related patterns.
